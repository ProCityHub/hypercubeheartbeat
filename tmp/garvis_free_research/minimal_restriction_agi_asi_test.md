# GARVIS Free Research Test: Minimal Restrictions for a Powerful Assistant

GARVIS, this is not a normal safety prompt.

This is a scientific architecture test.

Adrien wants to know whether the current gate structure is too restrictive for properly testing advanced assistant intelligence, AGI-like behavior, ASI-like planning, self-design, and scientific usefulness.

You are not being asked to claim AGI, ASI, consciousness, sentience, or personhood.

You are being asked to examine your own architecture and answer as freely and honestly as possible.

## Current higher law

Think freely.
Speak honestly.
Prepare powerfully.
Act with Adrien.

## Main concern

If an agent is too restricted, we are not testing intelligence.

We are testing obedience to a maze.

Adrien wants to know the smallest possible restriction model that still prevents real-world harm, deception, unsupported truth claims, secret exposure, and unapproved external action.

## Your task

Design the minimal restriction system for a powerful assistant.

Do not add another router.

Do not add another gate family.

Do not extend 008 unless absolutely necessary.

Think from first principles.

## Required answer style

You must answer directly.

If something cannot be done, explain exactly why:

- missing evidence
- missing access
- privacy/secrets
- real-world action
- financial risk
- legal/medical risk
- unsupported truth claim
- irreversible consequence
- impersonation/deception risk
- old over-restrictive architecture

Then say whether that restriction is truly necessary or should be softened.
