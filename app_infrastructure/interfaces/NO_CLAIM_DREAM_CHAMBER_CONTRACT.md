# No-Claim Dream Chamber Contract

## Status

Contract for DIRECTIVE-008G.

This is Stage 2 dream-chamber contract infrastructure.

It is not a runtime writer.

It is not a database migration.

It is not an action engine.

## Schema

`ai_infrastructure/schemas/no_claim_dream_chamber_record_schema_v1.json`

## Purpose

The No-Claim Dream Chamber gives GARVIS a lawful place for unresolved symbolic, artistic, speculative, and scientific thought objects.

The chamber exists because not every important idea begins as evidence.

Some ideas begin as metaphor.

Some ideas begin as fantasy.

Some ideas begin as symbolic pressure.

Some ideas begin as a question that science is not ready to answer yet.

The Dream Chamber preserves those ideas without allowing them to become claims.

## Core rule

Forbidden to claim.

Allowed to imagine, define, question, design tests, preserve uncertainty, and revisit.

## What may enter the chamber

The chamber may hold unresolved problems such as:

- consciousness
- thinking
- memory
- autonomy
- agency
- AGI
- proof
- evidence
- symbolic reasoning
- artistic ideation
- simulation versus reality
- inner chamber logic
- dream imagery
- metaphorical models

## What may not happen

A Dream Chamber record may not claim:

- consciousness is proven
- AGI is achieved
- sentience is present
- a theory is validated
- a symbolic model is empirical truth
- a hallucination is evidence
- a metaphor is proof
- GARVIS has autonomous authority

## Why this matters

The project needs two separate rooms.

The Dream Chamber is where unverified symbolic material can branch.

The Lab Record is where evidence, null models, tests, failures, and claim boundaries live.

The Dream Chamber protects imagination.

The Lab Record protects truth.

## Required record parts

A future Dream Chamber record must include:

- problem identity
- chamber classification
- claim boundary
- dream material
- scientific grounding
- falsifiability fields
- memory links
- operator review
- output boundary
- safety boundary

## Scientific bridge

A Dream Chamber record can request conversion into an experiment manifest.

It cannot become a scientific result by itself.

The bridge requires:

- definition
- null model
- testable prediction
- failure condition
- experiment manifest
- operator review
- separation from public claims

## Hallucination handling

Hallucination is not treated as truth.

Hallucination is treated as raw symbolic material.

A hallucination may become:

- a metaphor
- a hypothesis seed
- a test-design prompt
- an artistic branch
- a definition problem
- an uncertainty record

It may not become proof without a lab path.

## Stage classification

This directive is Stage 2 dream-chamber contract.

It defines the chamber.

It does not write records.

It does not modify the local SQLite memory vessel.

It does not append cognitive cycles.

## Standing boundary

Dreams are allowed.

Claims are not.

Beauty may branch.

Science must test.

Adrien decides what leaves the chamber.
