# Origins & Vision (Archived Layer)

These files are the project's original creative and symbolic layer —
the Lattice cosmology, sacred-binary explorations, and early bridge
concepts that started this work. They are preserved unedited because
this project's covenant keeps its history visible.

They are NOT validated claims, NOT working integrations, and NOT part
of the scientific record. For validated results see /OUTCOMES.md;
for withdrawn claims see /RETRACTIONS.md. The project's own book asks
of its founding axiom: "was it found, or was it installed?" — and
answers honestly: installed. The fire that started the forge is kept
here, fenced, and owned.
