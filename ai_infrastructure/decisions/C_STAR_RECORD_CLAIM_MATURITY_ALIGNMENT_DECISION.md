# C_star Record Claim Maturity Alignment Decision

## Decision

Human operator approved DIRECTIVE-008O: C_star Record Schema and Claim Maturity Viewer Alignment.

## Human operator

Adrien D Thomas

## Purpose

008O replaces blunt forbidden-claim record language with evidence-based claim maturity language.

The system may think, redefine, challenge, and preserve unproven ideas for later observation.

The system may not present unsupported material as truth.

## Core law

Nothing is forbidden to think.

Only unsupported truth presentation is blocked.

## Claim language

Use evidence-based statuses such as:

- raw_speculation
- not_claimable_yet
- mathematical_candidate
- testable_hypothesis
- provisional_operational_claim
- rejected

Do not use forbidden_to_claim as the primary claim status.

## Observation bank

Unproven ideas may enter a future observation bank as draft-only candidates.

Allowed actions:

- redefine
- challenge
- observe_later
- mature_with_evidence

The observation bank is not runtime memory append.

The observation bank is not proof.

The observation bank is not public claim.

## Viewer alignment

The cognitive-cycle viewer must accept newer deep-question cycles that use evaluation and standing_boundary fields instead of requiring only the older evolution_contract field.

## Boundaries

No autonomous action.

No automatic memory append.

No claim upgrade without evidence.

No public claim.

No lab result.

No experiment execution.

No network call.

No LLM call.

Adrien decides.
