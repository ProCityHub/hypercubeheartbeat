# C_star Record Claim Maturity Alignment Runbook

## Status

Implementation runbook for DIRECTIVE-008O.

## Purpose

008O aligns C_star records and viewer output with the new claim-maturity law.

The older phrase forbidden_to_claim is replaced by evidence-based status language.

The system may hold raw or unproven material as a future observation candidate.

## Correct interpretation

An unproven idea is not forbidden.

It is not_claimable_yet.

It may be redefined.

It may be challenged.

It may be observed later.

It may mature with evidence.

## Observation bank

The observation bank is a draft-only holding area inside a local record.

It does not append runtime memory.

It does not claim truth.

It does not execute anything.

It gives Adrien a place to preserve valuable raw ideas without making them public claims.

## Viewer alignment

The viewer should accept cycles that include evaluation and standing_boundary fields.

Those fields are part of newer C_star/deep-question cycles.

## Standing sentence

Do not kill the thought. Mature the claim.
