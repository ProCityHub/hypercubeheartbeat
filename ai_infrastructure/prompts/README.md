# Prompts

This folder stores approved prompts and directives for AI agents.

Allowed:

- Copilot directives
- Claude or Anthropic directives
- ChatGPT directives
- terminal workflow prompts
- review prompts
- safety prompts

Rules:

- Prompts may propose work.
- Prompts may not authorize scoring unless the directive explicitly says so.
- Prompts may not override frozen files.
- Prompts may not replace human approval.
